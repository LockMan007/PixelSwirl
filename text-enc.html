**To Reverse:** Copy the scrambled text from the output to the input, enter the **same key**, and click "Reverse".




This tool applies a combination of shuffling and XOR encryption to text. It first shuffles the characters based on a seeded random number generator (PRNG) derived from your key and number. It then applies an XOR operation to each character's code, also based on your key. This two-step process makes the text appear as an unreadable jumble of characters.
